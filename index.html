FAQ

Common questions.

Is ARPO really free? No hidden costs?

Yes — completely free. No subscription, no in-app purchases, no ads, no paywalled features. Everything is included. Free during beta and the plan is to keep core features free.

How is ARPO different from RP Hypertrophy or JeFit?

RP Hypertrophy charges ~$19.99/month and JeFit ~$7.99/month for auto-regulated training. ARPO delivers the same science — auto-progressive overload, RIR periodization, mesocycle programming — at zero cost. It also works 100% offline with no account needed, which neither competitor offers.

What is Auto-Regulated Progressive Overload (ARPO)?

ARPO is a training methodology where your program automatically adjusts volume, intensity, and weight based on how you actually performed — not a fixed schedule. After each set you rate your recovery, and the algorithm recalculates next week's targets. No guesswork, no plateau.

Does it work without an internet connection?

Yes. ARPO is 100% offline-first. Your entire exercise library, workout history, and training data live on your device. No account, no cloud sync, no internet required — ever.

Is Android supported?

Not yet. ARPO is currently iOS only, available via TestFlight beta. Android support is planned for a future release. Sign up to be notified when it launches.
